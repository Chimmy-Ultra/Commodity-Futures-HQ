Big Two Rules

Basics

4 players, 52 cards dealt evenly (13 each).
Goal: Be the first to play all your cards!

Card Ranking

2 > A > K > Q > J > 10 > 9 > 8 > 7 > 6 > 5 > 4 > 3

Suit Ranking

♠ > ♥ > ♦ > ♣

Combinations

Single — 1 card
Pair — 2 same rank
Triple — 3 same rank
5-card — Straight / Flush / Full House / Four of a Kind / Straight Flush

5-Card Hand Ranking

Straight Flush > Four of a Kind > Full House > Flush > Straight

Straights

Lowest: A-2-3-4-5   Highest: 10-J-Q-K-A
Wrap-around allowed (A can precede 2)

Gameplay

1. Player with ♣3 goes first (must include ♣3)
2. Next player must play same type but higher, or Pass
3. After 3 consecutive passes, last player leads freely
4. First to empty their hand wins!
